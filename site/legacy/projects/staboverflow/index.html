StabOverflow

2018

In August 2018, I deployed StabOverflow, an online Q&A forum for CS students at St. Anne's-Belfield (my high school).

Modeled after the widely-used Stack Overflow, StabOverflow allows students to ask questions, make comments, and respond with answers to CS-related problems. The idea was to facilitate interaction between members of the CS department across course / grade levels, and to construct, over time, a cumulative resource for all CS students to reference.

The live application can be found at overflow.cs.stab.org
