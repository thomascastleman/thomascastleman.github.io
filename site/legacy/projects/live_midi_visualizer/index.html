Live Music Visualizer

2018

In November of 2018, I was involved in a 3-week long course focused on technology, music, and visual art, during which I created this software, which translates input from a MIDI instrument into a visual display of colors and shapes.

The project took shape over several days and I documented my progress through video clips which I have pieced together in the above compilation. In the final clip, I play Debussy's Clair de Lune visualized with one of my favorite configurations.

The source is available here.
